The Model

AgriAI uses a MobileNetV2 convolutional neural network pre-trained on ImageNet and fine-tuned for plant disease classification. The architecture was chosen for its excellent accuracy-to-size ratio, making it fast for real-time inference on a CPU.

The model was trained for 5 epochs with early stopping, achieving 93% validation accuracy on the New Plant Diseases Dataset from Kaggle. Fine-tuning was done using the Adam optimizer with a learning rate of 1e-4 and data augmentation to improve generalization.

Dataset

The model was trained on the New Plant Diseases Dataset (Kaggle), a curated and augmented version of the PlantVillage dataset. It contains leaf images of healthy and diseased plants under controlled conditions.

The dataset contains 87,000+ RGB images split into 70,295 training and 17,572 validation images across 38 categories representing 14 crop species.

Disclaimer

AgriAI is an educational and research tool. While trained on a large dataset with high accuracy, AI predictions may not be 100% reliable in all real-world conditions.

For critical crop management decisions, always consult a certified agronomist or plant pathologist. Best results are obtained with clear, well-lit images of individual leaves showing visible symptoms.
